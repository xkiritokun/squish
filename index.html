Frequently asked questions

How do I compress an image without losing quality?

Set the quality slider to 75–85% for a great balance. Most images compressed at 80% look identical to the original but are 40–60% smaller in file size.

Is Squish really free?

Yes. There are no limits, no watermarks, and no signup required. Squish is completely free to use.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs in your browser using JavaScript. Your images are never sent anywhere.

What image formats are supported?

PNG, JPG/JPEG, and WebP. You can also convert between formats while compressing.

How much can I reduce an image's file size?

Typically 40–80% reduction depending on the original image and quality setting. JPGs compress more than PNGs on average.
