Frequently asked questions

How do I compress a file without losing quality?

Set the quality slider to 75–85% for a great balance. Most images and videos compressed at 80% look identical to the original but are 40–60% smaller in file size.

Is Squish really free?

Yes. There are no limits, no watermarks, and no signup required. Squish is completely free to use.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs directly on your device using JavaScript and WebAssembly. Your private files are never sent anywhere.

What file formats are supported?

For images: PNG, JPG/JPEG, and WebP. For videos: MP4, WebM, and MOV/Quicktime.

How does video compression work in the browser?

Squish uses a technology called FFmpeg.wasm, which runs a powerful video processing engine directly inside your browser without needing external servers.

All processing happens locally on your device. Your files are never uploaded to any server.
